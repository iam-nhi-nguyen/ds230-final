Clean At Sephora

Sephora is one of the foremost prestige beauty site on the internet, with around 300 brands on the whole website and over 2,700 skincare products alone. Each product's webpage is packed with information about the product, such as name, brand, price, category, and detailed description and list of ingredients. In addition, new customers can gain insight from others' purchases by looking at the number of Reviews (total number of customers who leave a review at the end of the webpage) and the number of Loves (total number of customers who recommended buying the product).

In 2018, Sephora lauched the Clean at Sephora seal as a response to an increase number of articles on questionable ingredients found in cosmetics that are either harmful for customers to use on their skin, or their process of harvesting causes environmental issues. Clean at Sephora products are claimed to not include a certain list of harmful ingredients (the full list can be found in the Data section). Clean at Sephoraseal is supposed to force brands to be transparent about their ingredient list and encourage customers to shop clean products.
